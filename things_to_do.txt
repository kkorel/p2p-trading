1. CDS (Catalog Discovery Service) - https://34.93.141.21.sslip.io/beckn/catalog
What it does: Central catalog of all energy offers from all sellers across the network.
How you use it:
- Your BAP calls POST /search to discover available energy offers
- CDS responds with on_search callback containing matching offers
- Integration complete: /api/discover calls external CDS

2. Ledger - https://34.93.166.38.sslip.io
What it does: Immutable multi-party record of all trades. Think of it as the "blockchain" of energy trades.
Three main endpoints:

POST /ledger/put       Your BAP (buyer platform)       On on_confirm - creates trade record
POST /ledger/record    DISCOMs only                    Record actual delivery/fulfillment
POST /ledger/get       Everyone                        Query trade records

Your integration:
- Write on on_confirm: When an order is confirmed, write to ledger
- Mock reads for now: When querying order status, mock the ledger response

3. Verifiable Credentials (VC) - https://open-vcs.up.railway.app/
What it does: Issues and verifies energy credentials (seller DER certificates, buyer eligibility, etc.)
The Google Drive (19EJLh4rLD8...) acts as a "wallet" containing issued VCs for testing.
